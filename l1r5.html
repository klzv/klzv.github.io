    L1R5 stands for "Language 1, Regular 5".

    L R R R R R
    Language Maths Science Humanities Any 2 other subjects
    excluding religious studies

    "Language 1" can only include 1 language (duh):

    • EL
    • MT
    • HMT

    Maths can include:

    • E Maths
    • A Maths

    Science can include

    • Comb. sci.
    • Bio
    • Chem
    • Phy

    Humanities can include:

    • Comb. humans.
    • Pure hist
    • Pure geog
    • Pure lit
    • Computing
    • ESS
    • AEP
    • MEP

    Other Subjects

    • Cannot include religious studies
    • Cannot include HMT or EL

    EL and HMT can only be used for Language. It cannot be used to fill in the remaining regular 2, and cannot be used in conjunction with your HMT.

    • If you put HMT (HCL, HML, HTL) as your Language, you cannot put your 'normal' MT as a remaining 2.
    • However, if you put English as your Language, then 'normal MT' can be used as a remaining 2, but not your HMT.

    Bonus points

    The maximum you can minus off is 4 points.
    This means that you can't stack more than 2 bonus point systems together.

    LEAPS 2.0 – Up to -2

    Higher Chinese

    To continue taking HCL in Sec 4: 'Pass' HCL in Sec 3.
    Some schools require students to get an A for Sec 3 CL to continue.

    To qualify for -2: C6 and above for both English and HCL

    To not take H1 CL in A Level: D7 and above

    Conversion of grades from HCL to H1 CL:
    • A1, A2 → A
    • B3 → B
    • B4 → C
    • C5 → D
    • C6 → E
    • D7 → S
    To quality for uni, minimum S grade (subpass) for H1 Mother tongue so HCL equivalent is D7.
